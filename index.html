Infant Mortality from 2016 -2020

Infant mortality is any death of a person under the age of 1 (Unicef). This dashboard will explore how infant mortality varies worldwide from 2016-2020. With this initial discovery, we get to what typically high infant mortality is commonly associated with and explore the impact Covid-19 has on infant mortality.

Map of the Infant Mortality Rate

From the map above, we can see that Africa had the highest infant mortality normalised values throughout the period. The other area of higher values was in the area surrounding India. But otherwise, the remaining countries had similar rates of infant mortality. It is also essential to know that there weren't massive amounts of changes for governments for the semester.
